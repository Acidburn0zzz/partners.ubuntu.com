Provide services with the backing of Canonical

Whether you’re a value-add reseller (VAR) or a systems integrator, the Ubuntu reseller programme provides valuable training, certification and, above all, direct access to the technologists at Canonical. This means you can deploy Ubuntu solutions for your customers with confidence.

What’s included?

As a reseller partner in the Ubuntu programme you can expect:

  • Opportunities to profit from Ubuntu Advantage support contracts
  • Technical training for your customer-facing team on the latest features of Ubuntu
  • The marketing benefits of certification
  • Permission to use the Canonical and Ubuntu trademarks in your own materials
  • Close collaboration and technical support from the engineers who develop Ubuntu
  • VIP invitations to Canonical events in your country
  • Inside information on forthcoming developments in Ubuntu

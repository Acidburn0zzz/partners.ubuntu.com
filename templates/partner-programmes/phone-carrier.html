Phone carriers

Ubuntu comes to phones with the specific needs of network operators, OEMs and ODMs in mind. It offers great performance on handsets with a low bill of materials, while opening up new opportunities for phone and PC convergence at the top end of the market. And there are many ways in which the user experience can be tailored to your brand.

Ubuntu is driving change in the mobile industry by delivering what matters to device manufacturers and operators — the only platform which allows ownership of the service layer, enabling unprecedented differentiation and revenue sharing opportunities.

What you get when you work with us

  • Stunning design on any smartphone
  • Superior customisation opportunities
  • Service layer ownership (user identity, billing, content, services, branding)
  • No platform fragmentation
  • Aligned with 4G/LTE investments: designed to grow service and data usage

Ubuntu phone partners

The Ubuntu Carrier Advisory Group (CAG) lets mobile operators shape Ubuntu’s mobile strategy. Members receive advance confidential briefings and provide us with industry insight to ensure that Ubuntu meets their needs.

Members of the CAG have the opportunity to be a launch partner and gain the right to ship Ubuntu in markets they serve. They also benefit from regular CAG meetings, wide industry participation and launch priority
